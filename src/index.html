Smart Widgets

Create embeddable widgets for your The Game Crafter games. These widgets feature your shop image, the title of the game, and a large 'buy now' button that links directly to TGC. Just select your game, customise your widget, and copy the code to your website or blog.

The widgets are built on top of the API, which means that if you change anything in your game settings, the information in your widget will also be automatically updated. You don't have to generate a new widget every time.
